About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, born on 5 November 1988, is one of the most iconic cricketers of all time. Known for his aggressive batting style, consistency, and leadership, he has carried Indian cricket forward in the modern era. Kohli’s journey from a passionate teenager in Delhi to becoming India’s cricketing backbone has inspired millions.

Career Highlights

  • 🏏 Over 26,000+ international runs across formats.
  • 🌍 Fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000 & 12,000 ODI runs.
  • 🏆 Captained India to a Test series victory in Australia (2018–19).
  • 🥇 Multiple ICC Cricketer of the Year awards.
  • 🎖️ Padma Shri Award in 2017.

Records & Achievements

  • 🔥 Most centuries in ODI cricket (50+ centuries).
  • ⚡ Fastest to 20,000 international runs in combined formats.
  • 🏏 Only player to average 50+ in all formats simultaneously.
  • 🥇 Leading run-scorer in T20 World Cups.

Personal Life & Inspirations

Off the field, Virat Kohli is known for his fitness regime, discipline, and philanthropy. He married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in 2017, and together they are one of India’s most admired celebrity couples. Kohli also runs the Virat Kohli Foundation, focusing on helping underprivileged children and supporting athletes.
